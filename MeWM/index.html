Abstract

Navigation World Model (NWM) is a controllable video generation model that predicts future visual observations from past visual input and navigation actions. It uses Conditional Diffusion Transformer (CDiT) to capture complex environmental dynamics and enables planning in both familiar and novel environments.

Limitations
