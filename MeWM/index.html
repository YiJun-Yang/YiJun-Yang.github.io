CVPR 2025 (Oral)

Authors: Amir Bar, Gaoyue Zhou, Danny Tran, Trevor Darrell, Yann LeCun

Abstract

Navigation World Models (NWM) predict future visual observations from past ones and actions using a Conditional Diffusion Transformer. They support planning in both known and novel environments.
