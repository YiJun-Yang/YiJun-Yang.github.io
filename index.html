Biography

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou) supervised by Prof. Lei Zhu and Dr. Huazhu Fu. I obtained my Bachelor degree with honors in Artificial Intelligence at School of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University in 2022. I also work closely with Dr. Angelica I. Aviles-Rivero, Dr. Shujun Wang and Prof. Carola-Bibiane Schönlieb at The University of Cambridge.

My research interests mainly lie in Medical Image Analysis, low-level vision.
