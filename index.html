Biography

I am currently an undergraduate student majoring in Artificial Intelligence in School of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University, supervised by Prof. Gan Tian. I am expected to get my Bachelor degree in June, 2022. I have the competitive background in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Computer Graphics, Robotics, etc.

My research interests mainly lie in Computer Vision, Domain Generalization, Multi-modality Learning and Video Understanding. Currently, I am working with Prof. Lequan Yu in The University of Hong Kong as a research intern. Our paper on Domain Generalization is coming soon!

"Keep Calm and Carry On!". I'm committed to being a self-motivated and comprehensive person. As a team player, I am much willing to collaborate and share with others.
