Biography

I am currently a Bachelor student in School of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University, majoring in Artificial Intelligence. I will get my Bachelor degree in 2022. My GPA is 88.8/100 now. I have the background in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Mining, Information retrieval, Robotics, Natural Language Process, Computer Graphics, etc.

I am also a research assistant in iLearn Lab , advised by Associate Prof. Tian Gan and co-advised by Prof. Liqiang Nie, the leader of iLearn. My research interest mainly lies in Multimedia, Computer Vision, Deep Learning, . At the moment, I am researching Video Understanding and Multimodal on Wechat Rhinoceros Project. Before that, I did ASL Detection with two other students. We made our own dataset about American Sign Language, which includes about 3830 pictures. ASL Detection can achieve real-time detection(55 frames per second) and good mAP@0.5:0.95(about 68%). More details can be found in Projects&Competitions.

"Keep Calm and Carry On!". To some extent, I am an all-round student who won't let self idle, which means that I will always find ways to improve my professional competence. As a team player, I am much willing to collaborate and share with each other.
